Grassroots

Abstract

For my MSc dissertation, I created a mobile app that attempts to solve a real-world problem in the youth football community (UK). Grassroots provides a platform for youth football coaches to easily schedule friendly games for their teams. The app allows coaches to host or search for available games in their teams' age and skill categories.

The project was suggested and backed by coaches of the England Youth FA (Sussex and Kent counties). Tech Stack: JavaScript, React Native, Node.js, Express, MongoDB
