TravelBuddy

Abstract

Solo travel is something most people want to experience and enjoy, but are too afraid to do because of being lonely, safety concerns, etc. That's why a couple of my classmates and I created TravelBuddy, a social network made specically for travelling. With TravelBuddy, you can find other travellers who are going to a place you want to go, and request to join them; on the other hand, if you're planning a solo trip, why not invite some of your Travel Buddies to join the fun?

The project was built using the Next framework on Typescript, with HTML and CSS for styling and Firestore and NoSQL as the databases.
