Grassroots (work-in-progress)

For my MSc dissertation project, I am currently working on an application that will solve a real-world problem in the youth football circles. The coaches and managers of teams at the grass-root level do not have an adequate method of communicating with each other, other than Whatsapp and Telegram. The goal of this application is to provide a platform for these individuals to be able to communicate and schedule games with other teams, to provide exposure to these budding football amateurs.

Note

The project is backed by coaches of the England Youth FA (Sussex and Kent counties). Tech Stack: React Native, Node.js, Express, MongoDB
